About Sunrise Bakery

Sunrise Bakery is a family-owned bakery specializing in artisan breads, sweet pastries, and small-batch coffee. We use locally sourced ingredients and bake every morning to bring you the freshest flavors.

Accessibility Information

We strive to make our bakery welcoming to everyone. Highlights for assistive technology users:

  • Clear, high-contrast signage and tactile markers at the entrance.
  • Wheelchair-accessible entrance and restroom.
  • Allergen information available on request; staff trained to assist customers with dietary needs.
